All time favorite game

10 facts you may not know about COD MW2

    Amazing Opening Sales:

  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 sales were incredible. Even Activision didn't expect the game to do as well as it did. It sold nearly 5 million copies within the first 24 hours of being available to consumers. Topping out to a approximate amount of 4.7 million! These sales came from mostly the United States and from the United Kingdom.

    • Worldwide Record Sales:

  • The first day sells were incredible. The final sales were just as mesmerizing with a staggering 14 million copies sold nationwide. Lauching this title to the second best selling video game in UK history

    • Most broken records recorded in a video game:

  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 online gameplay was one of the greatest of all time and to accompany this was the most records broken on a video game through DLC and overall multiplayer performance

    • Superb Soundtrack:

  • The soundtrack of Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2. Was composed by legend "Hans Zimmer". Hans is ranked as one of the best if not the greatest composers of all time. He has done many works ranging from "The Lion King" to "Pirates of the Carribeans"

    • Comic Book:

  • When Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 was released there was a comic series released with it as well called "Modern Warfare 2 Ghost". Ghost came in a 6 part series that was based off of the video game that did well in sales also.

    • Chicken Easter Egg:

  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 included many fun easter eggs. The most renouned of them all is the Tactical Nuke Chicken Easter Egg. This happened when you get a Tactical Nuke or another player gets one and calls it in. When calling in a tactical nuke there is are zero chance of survival but the chickens still remained alive in the levels even after the bombing.

    • Random Teddy bears:

  • When traveling around the maps in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2. You will see teddy bears scattered all over the map. You will find some hidden and you will find some right out in the open. This was probably a little secret that the developers wanted to have in the game.

    • Maps removed:

  • To make things even crazier. There were 12 maps that were removed from the game! these maps were removed before the game was released. This was probably due to time constraints on the developers. They didn't want to rush the development so they removed them from the project all together.

    • Multiply game shoutouts:

  • Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 was such a success that other games gave them a shoutout in their games. Some of the most known are Far Cry 3 and Battlefield Bad Company 2

    • Billion dollar milestone:

  • There are few games that pass the $1 billion dollar milestone and Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 is one of the games to do it. Only after a year did the game reach this amazing feat. This is why Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 is the greatest Call of Duty game of the franchise and also one of the greatest shooters of all time.
